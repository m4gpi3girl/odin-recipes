Simple Lasange Recipe

This wonderfully simple lasange recipe is perfect for people who don't have lots of time and resources to make a hearty dish.

With 9 easy ingredients, this dish will take around 30 mins to prepare and 30 mins to cook! Lets get stuck in.

Ingredients

  • onion (diced) and garlic (minced)
  • beef mince (minced)
  • tomato puree (pureed)
  • canned tomatos (canned)
  • beef stock cube (liquidized)
  • cheese (grated)
  • lasagne sheets (dried)

Steps

  1. Saute the onion and garlic in a large frying pan
  2. Add the beef mince and cook until browned
  3. Cook the saurce
    • add tomato puree, canned tomatoes, beef stock, herbs
    • bring to boil and simmer, stirring occasionally, for around 20 mins
  4. Assemble the lasagne
    • add a layer of sauce to the bottom of a baking dish
    • put lasagne sheets on top then cheese
    • repeat until finished
  5. Bake for 30-40 minutes / until cooked through and golden
